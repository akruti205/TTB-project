How It Works

Three steps. Complete picture.

01
🧤
Put on the Glove
Slip on your AgriSense Glove before heading to the field. It looks and works like a normal farming glove. Auto-connects to your phone via Bluetooth. No buttons, no setup, no complexity.
02
🌱
Touch Your Soil
Press your fingers into the soil as you normally would while working your field. Five sensors activate instantly on contact — reading moisture, NPK, pH, and temperature simultaneously in under 5 seconds.
03
📊
Get Your Answer
The LED strip flashes colour feedback instantly. Your phone shows full readings and AI recommendations. The voice assistant speaks advice in your language: "Apply urea today" or "No irrigation needed — rain in 2 days."
